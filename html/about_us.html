About Us

Miniworld it's a website of mini-games for La Sapienza University

Welcome to the 'About Us' page of our website project for La Sapienza University. We are three computer engineering students passionate about web development, and we decided to create a mini-game website to provide our visitors with a fun and engaging gaming experience. Our main goal is to offer our users a wide selection of entertaining and engaging games to satisfy every interest and preference

Our development team consists of three highly passionate members. We understand the importance of website usability and accessibility, which is why we have adopted the best design and development practices to ensure simple and intuitive navigation, without any complications. We are proud to present our mini-game website for La Sapienza University and hope that it will be appreciated by our visitors.
